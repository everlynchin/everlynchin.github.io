Our Story

SS Aluminum Glass Hardware Industries (M) SDN BHD is a competitive company that has established itself as a reliable and trusted specialist in Aluminium & Glass for various development projects, particularly in high-rise buildings. They are known for their expertise in aluminium cladding installation, offering innovative designs and professional technical know-how. With their technical proficiency in aluminium, glass, stainless steel, mild steel structure support, canopy, balcony, and more, they provide a comprehensive one-stop service to customers, including design, fabrication, and installation.
